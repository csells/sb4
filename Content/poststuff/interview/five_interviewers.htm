A Tale of Five Interviewers

Andrew Cherry just had an interview and posted his experience to the Windows Off Topic Mailing List:

I had five interviewers:

  • First: 3 Programming questions
  • Second: Explain my current major programming assignment at work (in detail), 2 programming questions
  • Third: 1 Logic Problem (reasonably simple, once I got my brain working), then 1 programming question
  • Fourth: Explain difference between arrays and linked lists and where you would use them (drawbacks to being a soon-to-be college grad), then five or six smaller programming questions
  • Fifth: ONE programming question in an hour interview. Then the hard stuff. *shudder*
