CRANBERRY SALSA

1 can diced tomatoes

1 can whole berry cranberries

1 pkg. Salsa Dip Stix

Stir well to combine. Refrigerate 1 hour. For an excellent appetizer, serve over cream cheese with tortilla chips for dipping.

GUACAMOLE

3 ripe avocados, diced

1 medium Roma tomato, diced

3 Tbsp. Lime juice

1/2 C. sour cream

1 tsp. salt

1 pkg. Salsa Dip Stix

Mash two of the avocados, mix with remaining diced avocado and tomato. Add lime juice, sour cream, salt and Salsa Dip Stix. Stir well to combine. Refrigerate 1 hour. Serve with tortilla chips.

(Try oven toasting a flour tortilla over an oven safe bowl to form a tortilla bowl. Serve guacamole in the tortilla bowl.)

 

GRILLED SALMON

1 pkg. Dill Dip Stix

Filet of Salmon

Lemon Juice

 

Preheat grill.  Mix Dill Dip Stix according to directions. Rinse and pat dry Salmon filet.  Make a shallow pan out of heavy duty aluminum foil. Place salmon in foil pan.  Spread a generous amount of Dill Dip Stix over salmon.  Add a small amount of lemon juice.  Place on a hot gill.  Grill about 20 minutes or until salmon flakes when lightly touched.  Remove from grill and serve at once.

 

GRILLED PORK CHOPS

1 pkg. Bacon Horseradish Dip Stix

6 - 1"thick sliced pork loin chops

3 Tbsp. oil

 

Lay out pork chops and sprinkle half of Bacon Horseradish Dip Stix over top of chops. Press into meat. Turn over chops and sprinkle remaining half of Bacon Horseradish Dip Stix over other side of chops. Press into meat.

Place chops into zip-lock bag and drizzle oil over meat. Seal bag and massage oil in gently. Place in refrigerator 4 hours or overnight. Grill!

 

POTATO SKINS

10 Medium-sized potatoes

2 Cups grated cheddar cheese

1 pkg. Bacon Dip Stix (made according to directions 1 cup Mayo & 1 cup sour cream)

Bake potatoes in oven for one hour at 350 degrees. When cool, cut in half and scoop out the middle of the potato leaving a little to make a firm shell (save the inside of the potato to make garlic mashed potatoes). Next deep fry the skins until brown ad crisp. Place on a cookie sheet, sprinkle shredded cheese on top and bake at 350 degrees until cheese melts. Remove from oven and serve hot with the Bacon Dip Stix mixture on top.

B L T’S MADE EASY

BACON SPREAD

1 pkg.. Bacon Dip Stix

8 oz. Container of sour cream

¼ cup chopped green olives

1 pkg.. Budding Corn Beef (chopped)

Mix together above ingredients and chill 30 min. before serving. Goes great with any kind of crackers.

DIP STIX TIP:

ADD A VARIETY OF COOKED VEGETABLES OR COOKED GROUND BEEF TO THE SKINS RECIPE. ALSO TRY THE BACON HORSERADISH FLAVOR IN BOTH OF THESE RECIPES FOR A LITTLE EXTRA KICK.

BACON CHEESE BALL

1 - 8oz. Pkg. of cream cheese

8 oz. shredded Monterey Jack cheese

1 tbsp. Margarine (opt)

1 pkg.. Bacon Dip Stix

Mix the softened cream cheese together with the Jack cheese, margarine and Bacon Dip Stix and form into a ball or any shape you want. Refrigerate 30 min. before serving with your favorite crackers.

DILL PICKLE HOR’DEURVES

1 – 12 oz. pkg.. of cream cheese

1 pkg.. Bacon Dip Stix

1 large jar of dill pickles

Cut pickles in half cross wise. Scoop out seeds and pat dry. Mix the softened cream cheese with the Bacon Dip Stix and place in a zip lock bag. Cut a small hole in one corner end. Gently squeeze the Bacon Dip Stix onto the pickle half. Chill and serve.

TWICE BAKED POTATOES

This one is fairly simple. First, bake a medium-sized potato for one hour at 350 degrees. While your tater is baking, make a batch of Bacon Dip Stix. When your tater is done cut it in half after it has cooled a bit. Scoop out the meat of the potato leaving enough to keep the tater from breaking apart. Mash tater innards with a little warm milk, a little butter and a scoop or two of Bacon Dip Stix. Add your tater mix back into the shell, top with shredded cheese (opt.) and put them into the oven until heated through. Top with a spoon full of Bacon Dip Stix.

Dip Stix Tip:

Bacon Horseradish Dip Stix can be substituted for any of the above recipes. Also great served with Prime Rib, Pork Roasts or Chops. Give it a try!

How about trying Bacon Horseradish in your favorite potato salad, gives a whole new meaning to flavor!

DILL-RANCH-DRESSING

2 Cups low-fat Mayo

1 Cup skim milk

½ tsp.. Worcestershire sauce

1 pkg.. Dill Dip Stix

Sour the milk with 1 tsp.. Lemon juice or white vinegar and add to the Mayo, Worcestershire and Dill Dip Stix, mix will. The thickness of the dressing can be adjusted with more or less milk.

Variation: Substitute a Bacon Dip Stix for bacon dressing and add diced tomatoes for bacon tomato dressing.

DILL POTATO SALAD

This recipe is way to easy to be this good!

1 batch Dill Dip Stix mix according to package directions.

2 pounds "B" sized red potatoes

Quarter potatoes with the skin on and boil to your desired doneness. Add potatoes to your already prepared dip mix and stir gently (don’t bust up the spuds). That’s it! If this is to easy, add some diced red/green peppers for color and flavor or radishes or celery, Whatever! Be creative and put in your favorite goodies.

VEGGIE PIZZA

1 – 8 oz. pkg.. Cream cheese (softened)

½ Cup Mayo

½ Cup sour cream

1 heaping Tbsp.. Dill Dip Stix

1 Tbsp.. Garlic Garlic Dip Stix

Mix ingredients together and set aside. Then you will need the following.

2 pkgs. of crescent rolls

Variety of vegetables

Spread crescent roll dough out on a cookie sheet. Bake as directed on the roll dough package and cool. Dice your vegetables such as black olives, tomatoes, cucumbers, green and or red peppers, onions, etc… (You can even add some cooked pepperoni slices if you like) Spread cream cheese mixture on pre-baked crust, sprinkle veggies on top and finish with some shredded cheddar cheese.

GARLIC-MASHED-POTATOES

4 Medium sized potatoes, washed and quartered (leave the skins on)

½ Cup milk

1 Tbsp. butter or margarine

1 pkg. Garlic Garlic Dip Stix

Boil your spuds and drain water. Put the milk in the same pan (we’re saving dish washing time here) with the butter and Garlic Garlic Dip Stix. When the milk and goodies are heated through, add the spuds and mash.

Optional: Add a little sour cream to the spuds when you mash them, GREAT!!!

CLASSIC ONION TIPS:

Are you thinking of having some type of roast for dinner? Before you pop it in the oven, try this. Rub a small amount of cooking oil on the top and sides of the roast, then sprinkle a Classic Onion Dip Stix all over the roast. Rub it in good, then roast as usual. The onion mix and the juice’s in the bottom of the pan will combine to make a very tasty Au Jus or gravy and your roast will have a crispy, onion flavored coating.

CLASSIC ONION TIPS

Add 1 pkg. Classic Onion to a 2 cup batch of plain white rice. Just add the mix to the water while the rice is cooking. This will turn plain white rice into a tasty side dish with a zip of onion.

MEATLOAF

1 – ½ pound ground beef

2 Eggs

Combine ingredients in a bowl and mix together until blended well. Form into a loaf and place in a greased loaf pan and bake for 45 min. at 350 degrees.

SALSA TIPS:

If you like a milder salsa, use half a packet to a can of tomatoes or add more tomatoes to a full pack. For hotter salsa, try a can of diced tomatoes with green chilies such as Rotel brand.

For added flavor and spice, add some dry Salsa Dip Stix mix to your taco meat while it’s cooking.

Keep a batch of Salsa Dip Stix made-up in the refrigerator for everyday cooking. Use it anywhere you’d use canned or fresh diced tomatoes such as soup, stew or pasta sauces for an interesting spicy taste.

NACHO EVERYDAY NACHOS

For these nachos, you have to cook up a couple of items first (believe me it’s worth the effort).

First, make the spicy cheese dip from the other page. Next, brown 1 pound of ground beef and drain. While the beef and cheese dip is cooking, chop some onions and tomatoes (these are optional).

Now get a big platter and mound it with tortilla chips, then sprinkle the cooked ground beef on top of the chips. Pour the heated cheese dip over the top of the beef chips, throw on your optional diced onions and tomatoes and EAT!!!

SALSA ROAST CHICKEN

Prepare a batch of Salsa Dip Stix according to pkg. directions. Arrange cut-up chicken pieces in a shallow baking pan, cover with salsa mixture and bake as usual. Half way through the cooking time, turn chicken pieces to re-coat with salsa mixture. This works great with chicken wing also.

SESAME TIPS:

Mix Sesame Dip Stix with softened cream cheese, makes a wonderful spread for toasted bagels.

Sprinkle Sesame Dip Stix over fish filets in an aluminum pan, before grilling add a little butter, some fresh mushrooms and a little onion. Grill 10 min just before done add a few capers and wedges of tomato. Can’t be beat!!!

SPINACH TIPS:

While the SPINACH Dip Stix makes a great vegetable dip, one of the favorite ways to eat the dip is with bread. Try a loaf of Sourdough or Hawaiian (the round loaf) cut a circle in the loaf making a bowl out of the bread and put the SPINACH Dip Stix (prepared) in the center. Break up the bread you cut from the middle of the loaf and eat the dip with the bread chunks.
